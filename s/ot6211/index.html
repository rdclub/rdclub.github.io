Once upon a time there was one school inside a village. It was a school full of nice and happy students.

One day the teacher saw a student that was green. She asked to him the reason he was green. He answered:

I'm a normal student like others!

The teacher felt something inside her. She just said "Interesting.." and left.

One day, the teacher saw the green boy again. She asked "Are you even from Earth?"

He said "I don't know what Earth is but I'm from Venus.

She got a bit scared and went to the lodging without giving an answer.

Days later, most of the teachers were talking about the green boy. The green boy got sad. He wanted an end of this.

While he was in class, he said "Stop talking about me, it makes me sad. I just like being here." and left.

Everyone understood their mistake as well as the teacher. They said sorry and became very good friends with The Green Boy.
